# Findings

This folder is the institutional output of the On-Device AI Coding engagement.

**Audience:** Corporate colleagues, SMEs, managers, citizen developers, and technology enthusiasts who want to understand what on-device AI coding means in practice — without having gone through the engagement themselves.

**How it's built:** Participants fill these documents progressively during the engagement via Pull Requests, drawing from their direct experience across different hardware tiers and use cases. It's co-authored evidence, not a single author's opinion.

## Reading Order

**If you're an IT professional deciding whether to try this:**  
Start with [HARDWARE-TIERS.md](HARDWARE-TIERS.md), then [USE-CASE-MATRIX.md](USE-CASE-MATRIX.md).

**If you're a manager or SME evaluating the concept:**  
Read [ASSESSMENT.md](ASSESSMENT.md) directly — it's written for you.

**If you want to replicate this setup:**  
Go to [DEPLOYMENT-TEMPLATE.md](DEPLOYMENT-TEMPLATE.md), then follow [QUICKSTART.md](../QUICKSTART.md).

## How to Contribute (Participants)

Each weekly submission's `NOTES.md` feeds directly into the tables and observations in this folder. After your Week 1–3 PRs are merged, come back here and add your observations to the relevant sections. Week 4 is when the group synthesizes ASSESSMENT.md together.

> Contributions here are PRs like any other. Keep entries factual and grounded in what you actually experienced — no speculation.
