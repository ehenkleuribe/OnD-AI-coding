# Week 4 Submissions — MVP Challenge

**Challenge:** Deliver a functional application or tool, demo-able in 10 minutes.  
Full details → [CHALLENGES.md](../../CHALLENGES.md#-week-4--mvp-challenge)

> Scope confirmed at the live kickoff session. Coordinate with your partner (Duo) or work Solo.

---

## What to Submit

Create a folder: `submissions/week-4/your-github-username/` (or `your-duo-usernames/` for pairs)

Include:
- Your application / tool files (or a link to them if too large)
- A `README.md` *inside your folder* with: what it is, who it's for, how to run it
- A demo link or recording (screen share recording, video, or live demo at kickoff)
- `NOTES.md` — completed from the template below
